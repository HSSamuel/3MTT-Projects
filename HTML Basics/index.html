Hello! Welcome to West African Management Development Institutes Network (WAMDEVIN)

WAMDEVIN is a sub-regional Network of Management Development Institutions and Business schools focused on human resource building, management consultancy, research, and publications in Anglo-phone West African countries. The countries include Nigeria, Ghana, Sierra Leone, The Gambia, Liberia, and Cameroon, with its Secretariat in Nigeria.
